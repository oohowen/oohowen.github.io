Project
idea

This project is based on the previous piano idea. On top of that, we added bluetooth control and a more convenient power DC battery power supply. Softwarewise, RTOS was utilized to further eliminate the delay between the input and the output.

setup

Parts include mbed, touch keypad, bluetooth module, LCD display, audio amplifier, speaker, DC battery, relay and voltage regulator.
